# Contributor Setting Guide

This document will guide you through the process of adding your information to the project's contributor list, ensuring your contributions are properly recorded and displayed.

## Contributor Information Setup Steps

### Step 1: Add Your Information to the Contributor List

Edit the **`custom/info/contributors.json`** file, following the existing data format to add your information.

### Step 2: Upload Avatar (Optional)

(Optional) Place your avatar image in the **`app/assets/avatar/contributors`** directory.

### Complete the Setup

After completing the above operations, your information will automatically appear in the contributor list on the homepage!
